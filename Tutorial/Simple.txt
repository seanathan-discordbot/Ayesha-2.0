`/start <character name>` to create a character
`/profile` to view your character
`/pve <level>` to fight enemies
`/inventory` to view your weapons
`/equip` to equip a weapon
`/summon` to get new weapons and acolytes
`/tavern list` to view your acolytes
`/tavern equip` to equip an acolyte (you can have 2 equipped)

Read the `/tutorial` with topic 'Quick-Start Guide' to get more in-depth explanations of these commands!"
